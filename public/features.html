Bot Configuration Options

Message History: Configure how many previous messages your bots should remember for context. This helps your bots maintain better conversations by understanding recent chat history.

Personality & Tone: Set your bot's personality and communication tone. These are optional - leave them empty to rely on your Knowledge Management settings instead. If both are set, personality and tone options will take priority over knowledge-based personality settings.

Reply to DMs: Control whether your bot can respond to direct messages. For privacy, your bot will never initiate a conversation.

Response Language: Select the language your bot will use for all responses. The bot will understand messages in any language but will always reply in the selected language only.

Sentence Length: Control how long your bot's responses should be. Choose between 1-4 sentences for concise replies, select "Long" for detailed responses (5+ sentences), or enable the new Dynamic Length option to let the bot decide the ideal response length for each message.

Special Users: Manage special users who will receive priority recognition and personalized responses. Go to Special Users Management →
