Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Discord OAuth Information

When you log in with Discord, we collect:

  • Your Discord username and discriminator
  • Your Discord user ID (for account linking)
  • Your Discord avatar URL
  • Your email address (if public on your Discord profile)

1.2 Service Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our service:

  • Bot configurations and settings
  • Knowledge base entries you create
  • API usage statistics
  • Error logs and debugging information

1.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • IP addresses (for security and analytics)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Session data and cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Discord bot hosting service
  • Authenticate and secure your account
  • Process your bot configurations and requests
  • Provide customer support and troubleshooting
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. What We DON'T Do

Important Commitments:

  • We do NOT use your data to train AI models
  • We do NOT sell your personal information
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties for marketing
  • We do NOT store your Discord bot tokens in plain text
  • We do NOT access your bot conversations without permission

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our service (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Safety: To prevent harm to users or our platform

5. Data Security

We implement multiple security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication using Discord OAuth2
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

6. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Bot configurations: Until you delete them or your account
  • Usage logs: For up to 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Security logs: For up to 1 year for security monitoring

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your bot configurations
  • Withdrawal: Revoke consent for data processing

8. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Discord: For authentication and bot hosting
  • AI Providers: For bot intelligence (data is not stored by them)
  • Hosting Services: For infrastructure (under data processing agreements)

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs their data handling.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Basic analytics (anonymized)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes through our platform or email.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us through our support channels or dashboard.

Remember: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through your dashboard settings.
